About the Event

Winnipeg's First Of Its Kind

Interhouse Sports is a one-day community event where competition meets culture. Rooted in friendly rivalry and team spirit, it brings together people from all walks of life to compete in track, field, and street-style games — backed by live DJs, food, and nonstop vibes. Whether you're here to run, cheer, or just catch the energy, there's a house for you.
